Being a parent can be brutal. It requires you to be on your toes and constantly on the ball. To be honest, sometimes you can be the greatest parent in the world and still not have your children turn out how you thought. You've got to just keep trying and hoping.

Ingredients for (Probably) Passing the Test of Parentship

  • A healthy amount of tolerance
  • 1 support network
  • A smidge of childishness
  • A smidge of strictness
  • A whole lot of love

Steps

  1. Prepare as much as you can for the journey ahead.
  2. Put as much time as you can into your kids.
  3. Teach them everything from the mundane to the complex.
  4. Go have fun with them and show them how great life can be.
  5. Give them the space they desire as they get older, but always be there when needed.

* Parentship is really in the dictionary. I know parenthood is better but I didn't want to break the "ship" theme.
