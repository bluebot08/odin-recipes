Being a Good Friend Is the Key to Success

Everyone wants to have good friendships. The best way to make that happen is by being the best friend you can be. Try to be someone who always has an open ear and has your friend's best interests at heart.

Ingredients for Being a Good Friend (May Not Be Accurate)

  • Flexibility
  • Understanding
  • Patience
  • An Open Attitude
  • Empathy

Steps

  1. Show a true interest in your friend.
  2. Get to know them.
  3. Make them laugh when you can.
  4. Help them when you can
  5. Stick around when it really counts
